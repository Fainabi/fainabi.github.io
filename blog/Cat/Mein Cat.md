# Mein Cat : A Categorical Mind

:::meta
tags : blog, toy
attributes : pinned
:::

**Mein Cat** is a playful double entendre: a nod to *Mind* and a wink to *Category Theory*.

This blog is about seeing cognition, logic, and thought itself through the lens of category theory — not as dry formalism, but as a language for how we structure, connect, and make sense of the world.

Here, we explore the categorical mind:
how patterns of thinking arise from composition, universals, adjoints, and universal constructions.
One idea, one arrow, one insight at a time.
