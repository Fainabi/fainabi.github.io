# Category Theory

Category theory is a very beautiful language, for describing objects and their connectivity. Type theory (and homotopy type theory, HoTT) gives another local language depicting the development and proofs of other descriptions. For detailed and comprehensive understanding of these domains, I recommend [nLab](https://ncatlab.org), publications of [Emily Riehl](https://math.jhu.edu/~eriehl/), and the [HoTT](https://homotopytypetheory.org/book/) book.

Instead of tutorial or detailed reference of the category theory, I would only record some interesting points and views while learning it. For depth, width, and completeness, AI does better. Therefore, the valuable things are my felling and learning experience, that are not sampled from the existence digital materials, but condensed from yet another human view.

One of my conclusion of category theory is that, it is a language of languages, and a view of views. AI agrees to it as well.
