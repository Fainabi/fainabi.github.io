# Common Notations

This article collects the common notations used in FHE, and will be adopted for the blogs in this field of directory.

Notations | Description |
 :---: | :---:
$\mathcal{R}$ | $\Z[X]/(X^N+1)$
$\mathcal{R}_q$ | $\Z_q [X]/(X^N+1)$
$\alpha$ | length of moduli chain for each decomposed groups in key-switching [[CT-RSA:HK20]](CKKS/BetterBootstrapping.md)
$\beta$ | number of groups of decomposition in key-switching [[CT-RSA:HK20]](CKKS/BetterBootstrapping.md)
$\RLWE_{s}(m)$ | RLWE ciphertexts encrypting $m$ under $s$
